    Description

    Turn up the flavor on your coffee with the Cuisinart 14 Cup Programmable Coffeemaker! Choose a regular or bold flavor profile and set the brew-time up to 24 hours ahead so your next cup is ready when you are. The backlit LCD is easy-to-read, a ready tone can be turned on or off, and an indicator light signals when it’s time to decalcify.

    Engineered to produce the perfect brew, cup after cup, this coffeemaker combines extra-large capacity with fully programmable convenience. Designed with an array of custom settings, from 24-hour Brew Start to adjustable Auto-Off, it brews up to 14 cups into a dripless glass carafe. Choose regular or bold flavor and high, medium or low carafe plate temperature. Enjoy fresh tasting coffee, with a charcoal filter that eliminates water impurities.
